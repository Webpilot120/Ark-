The Mission

The mission of the Celestial Church of Christ, Ark of Covenant Parish, is to fulfill the divine instruction given from the foundation — to be a spiritual refuge, a place of worship, and a house of prophetic power. We exist to lead souls into the presence of God, build lives on the foundation of truth, and raise men and women who are spiritually grounded, prophetically alert, and eternally focused.

This Parish stands to reflect the light of Christ in a dark world. Our mission is to preach the undiluted word of God, to bring healing to the broken, direction to the lost, and revival to the weary. We are called to nurture a community where holiness, humility, and heaven-mindedness are not suggestions, but a way of life.

We are committed to raising altars that transform lives, producing true worshippers who worship in spirit and in truth, and preparing the saints for the coming of the Lord. Our assignment goes beyond our four walls — we are sent as vessels of light to spread the fire of the gospel throughout Nigeria and the world, with love, integrity, and spiritual authority.
