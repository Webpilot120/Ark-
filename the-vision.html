The Vision

The vision of the Celestial Church of Christ, Ark of Covenant Parish, is to become a globally recognized altar of divine encounter — a spiritual lighthouse built on prophecy, purity, and power. We see a Church that is not just structured in walls, but rooted in spirit, where heaven meets earth and God’s voice speaks without interruption.

We envision a sanctuary where the prophetic thrives, where visions are birthed and destinies are unveiled, where healing, deliverance, and supernatural intervention are not rare but expected. A Church where children, youths, and elders walk in purpose, and the presence of God is tangible.

Our vision is to raise a generation of worshippers and spiritual leaders who will uphold the tenets of the Celestial Church and shine as light to the nations. We see this Parish growing beyond borders, impacting lives locally and globally, yet remaining grounded in humility, obedience, and spiritual fire.

The Ark of Covenant Parish shall stand as a testimony that when God speaks, He performs. And when He sends, He provides — until His name is glorified across the ends of the earth.
