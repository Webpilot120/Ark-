The Mandate

The mandate upon the Celestial Church of Christ, Ark of Covenant Parish, is rooted in divine instruction and confirmed through prophetic utterance. It was not the ambition of man, but the command of the Most High God. From the moment the vision was declared through Prophetess Kemi Pasca in 2001, it was made clear that this Parish was not to be just another church but a spiritual assignment — a place chosen by God for His name, His presence, and His glory.

God’s instruction was simple yet profound: He would give a land where His house must be built. A sanctuary where the weary would find rest, where His Spirit would move freely, and where prophetic destinies would be birthed and fulfilled. This was later confirmed by multiple prophetic encounters in Abuja, each pointing toward one purpose — the establishment of His dwelling along Airport Road, a location revealed by divine vision before it was ever physically known.

The mandate is clear — to raise an altar unto the Lord, build a church founded on divine obedience, and preserve the purity of the Celestial order. It is a call to spiritual excellence, to stand as a lighthouse in the North, and to fulfill heaven’s assignment without compromise. We are not gathered here by accident, but by divine appointment — to be custodians of prophecy and carriers of God’s presence in this generation.
