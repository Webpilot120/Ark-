About Us

Celestial Church of Christ Ark of Covenant Parish is a vibrant Christian family built on worship, prayer, and the manifest power of God. It is a household of light, grace, and divine encounters where destinies are lifted and lives are transformed. God continues to enlarge the Ark of Covenant Parish, even as His glory is revealed in mighty ways through His servant – Prophet Oluseyi Adenuga.
