Our Divine Target

The Goal

The ultimate goal of the Celestial Church of Christ, Ark of Covenant Parish, is to fulfill God’s divine purpose on earth by raising a consecrated people, prepared for the return of Christ. Our goal is not driven by popularity or numbers, but by transformation — to see souls saved, lives changed, and destinies aligned with God’s perfect will.

We aim to be a center of deep spiritual growth and revival, where every member understands their calling, walks in holiness, and serves God with passion. Our goal is to preserve the sacred order of the Celestial Church while remaining sensitive to the leading of the Holy Spirit in a changing world.

We strive to be a Church that equips saints for kingdom work, raises leaders of integrity, nurtures the prophetic gift, and provides a safe spiritual haven for all. From children to elders, every soul matters. Every gathering, every prayer, every act of service is toward one end — that Christ may be glorified, and the Kingdom of God expanded through us.

Until the earth is filled with the knowledge of the Lord, as the waters cover the sea — we press toward the mark, steadfast, faithful, and focused on eternity.
